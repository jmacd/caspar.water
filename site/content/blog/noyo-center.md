Caspar Water is volunteering with our partner, the
[Noyo Marine Center](https://noyocenter.org/), to host its public water
quality monitoring portal.

Noyo Marine Center collects water quality data from multi-parameter
probes at its Field Station dock in Noyo Harbor, measuring dissolved
oxygen, salinity, water temperature, and tide level. Using the same
open-source [Watertown](/software.html) software that runs Caspar
Water's own monitoring, we now collect, archive, and publish that data
to a public portal so the community can follow the health of the harbor.

See the live data at
[casparwater.us/noyo-harbor](https://casparwater.us/noyo-harbor/).
