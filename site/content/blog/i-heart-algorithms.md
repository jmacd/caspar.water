My kids came home from school one day super excited about an exercise
where you have to count all the combinations of 5, 10, and 25 cent
coins that make a dollar. There's an algorithm for that and I wanted
to show it to them.

I wanted to illustrate the algorithm, not to make a slide deck. The
idea was I would write a description for the slides and let the
machine play artist. Sol LeWitt the conceptual artist had this idea.

Here is [an essay about a slide
deck](https://github.com/jmacd/algorithms-side-quest/blob/main/README.md)
for a [presentation about counting
change](https://jmacd.github.io/algorithms-side-quest/1). A student
asked how I made the animations. I wrote them up!
