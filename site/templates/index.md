Welcome to the Caspar Water Company website. Caspar Water is a small water system serving the historic village of Caspar on the Mendocino coast of California.

## Public Data Portal

We publish our water system monitoring data for transparency and community awareness. All readings are collected through OpenTelemetry Collectors and exported as interactive charts.

| Instrument | Description |
|------------|-------------|
| [**Well Depth**](/data/well-depth.html) | Height of the water column in the well |
| [**Tank Level**](/data/tank-level.html) | Treated water in the concrete storage tank |
| [**System Pressure**](/data/system-pressure.html) | Distribution system pressure |
| [**Chlorine Level**](/data/chlorine-level.html) | Chlorine tank inventory |
| [**pH**](/data/ph.html) | Treated water pH |

See the [Well Depth History](/well-depth-history.html) page for an annotated multi-year timeline showing leaks, repairs, and seasonal patterns.
